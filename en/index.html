Atlas is in the planning phase

The project is just getting started: there is hardly any content yet, we cannot promise ongoing releases, and we are securing funding and partners before testing and developing the modules.

What is Atlas?

A framework that carries ideas into impact

Atlas helps young people, communities, and public and private services rally around a shared goal. It connects people, methods, and language so new ideas can grow and be tested quickly.

Everything will live openly in the documentation. Each module is designed to work on its own or as part of a longer journey, but the content is still being planned and will be published only once we have the resources to test and develop it in the documentation.

The common thread is active youth participation and safe facilitation ‚Äì on their terms.

Why it works

  • Builds a shared language across sectors.
  • Puts youth perspectives first.
  • Offers ready-to-use tools and sessions.
  • Adapts to teams of any size.

Open to everyone

Atlas is free to use. The documentation is the book for the framework and will be updated as content becomes available.
